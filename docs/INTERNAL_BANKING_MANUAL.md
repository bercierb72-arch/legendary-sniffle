## Account Management

### Account Types
- **Operating Accounts**: Day-to-day operational funds
- **Reserve Accounts**: Emergency liquidity reserves
- **Investment Accounts**: Long-term asset management
- **Client Accounts**: Customer-facing banking services

### Account Setup
1. Submit account creation request
2. Complete KYC verification
3. Establish initial funding
4. Configure access permissions
5. Activate account

### Account Closure
1. Review outstanding obligations
2. Clear all pending transactions
3. Transfer remaining funds
4. Archive account records
5. Close account formally

## Transaction Processing

### Standard Transactions
- **Domestic Transfers**: Local fund transfers
- **International Transfers**: Cross-border payments
- **Wire Transfers**: Direct bank transfers
- **Scheduled Payments**: Recurring transactions

### Transaction Limits
- Single transaction limit: $1,000,000 USD
- Daily account limit: $5,000,000 USD
- Monthly limit: $50,000,000 USD
- Special approval required for amounts exceeding limits

### Authorization Matrix

| Amount Range | Required Approvers | Processing Time |
|---|---|---|
| < $100K | Supervisor | Same day |
| $100K - $500K | Manager + Compliance | 1 business day |
| $500K - $1M | Director + CFO | 1-2 business days |
| > $1M | Board approval required | 2-5 business days |

## Compliance and Controls

### Regulatory Requirements
- Anti-Money Laundering (AML) compliance
- Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures
- Transaction monitoring
- Sanctions screening
- Reporting obligations

### Internal Controls
- Segregation of duties
- Four-eye principle for high-value transactions
- Daily reconciliation
- Regular audits
- Access logging and monitoring

### Documentation
- Maintain transaction records for 7 years
- Preserve audit trails
- Document all approvals
- Store compliance certifications
- Archive communication logs

- Performance metrics

## Emergency Procedures

### System Outage
1. Activate backup systems
2. Notify stakeholders
3. Implement manual processing
4. Maintain audit trail
5. Communicate recovery timeline

### Unauthorized Access
1. Immediately isolate affected accounts
2. Notify compliance and security teams
3. Begin forensic investigation
4. Activate incident response protocol
5. Communicate with affected parties

### Data Breach
1. Activate data protection protocol
2. Assess scope and severity
3. Preserve evidence
4. Notify regulatory authorities
5. Execute communication plan

### Critical Infrastructure Failure
1. Declare emergency mode
2. Activate disaster recovery
3. Initiate failover procedures
4. Maintain critical operations
5. Execute recovery plan
